Joanna Majithia is a Nutritional Therapist practising in Brighton, Sussex.

Joanna offers a wide range of testing and therapy services at the Boyne House Nutrition Clinic. She helps clients with all kinds of health problems and specialises in digestive disorders, hormonal imbalances and adrenal fatigue.

Nutritional Therapy can help with a wide range of health issues including digestive problems, those suffering from fatigue and low energy, women with hormonal problems, those wanting to lose weight, and babies and children with allergies.

Read more about the benefits of nutritional therapy...

Joanna trained at the The Institute for Optimum Nutrition (ION), is a fully insured member of the The British Association for Nutritional Therapists (BANT) and is registered with The Complementary & Natural Healthcare Council (CNHC).

Joanna works closely with clients to conduct a holistic assessment of their nutritional needs and to compile an individual prescription for diet and supplementation.
