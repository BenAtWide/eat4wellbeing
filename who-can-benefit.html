Nutritional Therapy can benefit everyone, each person’s nutritional needs are different depending on inherited genes, age, nutritional deficiency, environment, lifestyle and levels of stressors. Nutritional therapy takes into account all these variants. During a confidential consultation a personalized diet and supplement programme with be designed for you which will be explained and followed up at agreed intervals. Joanna specializes in digestive health and successfully consults people with a wider range of health problems…

  • People suffering from digestive problems – including candida infections, constipation, diarrhea, heartburn, IBS, Crohn’s & ulcerative colitis and diverticular disease. Solving digestive disorders is key to achieving optimum health!
  • People with fatigue and low energy – stress and fatigue are common complaints that can be significantly improved with simple dietary changes.
  • Women – wanting relief from hormonal problems such as PMS, the menopause, endometriosis and fibroids.
  • Those wanting to lose weight – an easy-to-follow weight-management programme employs safe and effective ways to boost metabolism and balance your body’s natural weight permanently.
  • Couples starting a family – the right balance of nutrients and correct diet may help increase fertility, ease morning sickness, give increased energy during pregnancy and result in a healthier baby.
  • Babies – incl. infantile eczema, colic and irritability. Advice on diet during breastfeeding and correct weaning can be given to improve the baby’s health, reduce allergic tendencies and boost immunity.
  • Children – incl. allergies, eczema, asthma, glue ear, digestive problems, learning difficulties or hyperactivity.
